Streetwear

Streetwear is a type of fashion that has a fanbase majority of teenagers and youths under the age of 30.
This style is defined as the popular clothing worn by followers of popular cultures, for example: hip-hop
music culture, skate boatding, and other types of activity that fit into the theme of a "reckless youth".
Having so many different types of culture influencing it, it becomes hard for fashion industry to put it
into speccific range. The most impoertant thing is for you to set your thoughts free and show off exactly
the way you believe will make you look cool enough

A style that was born out of the New York City
hip-hop culture from the last 70s to early 80s with elements of
Los Angeles surf culture, streetwear took inspiration from the
do-it-yourself aesthetic of punk, Japanese street fashion, new wave, heavy metal
