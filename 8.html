DIYs

DIY, short for "do it yourself" DIY means that, instead of hiring a professional to do a particular
task or buying factory-done items, you're choosing to do that task or create those products yourself
by crafting with available everyday materials. That doesn't mean you can't turn to resources for help
Most people rely on YouTube tutorial, a book, or a blog post to find directions or get the DIY project
done, and it still counts as doing it yourself!
